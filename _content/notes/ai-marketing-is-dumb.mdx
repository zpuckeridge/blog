AI-generated marketing is like a toddler with a thesaurus and a Red Bull addiction trying to sell you a used car. It’s loud, it’s chaotic, it’s full of buzzwords it doesn’t understand, and you’re left wondering if it’s trying to scam you or just genuinely confused about what it’s selling.
